Top three activites in Leira

Attractions

Both cultural and nature attractions can be found throughout the region and the Valdres Guide is a great way to plan your stay. This guide can be found in all our tourist offices, our member businesses as well as online.

Activities

There are a large number of activities to chose from, throughout all four seasons. Traditional activities such as skiing, cycling and hiking are still the most popular, but there is an increasing demand for other types of activities such as fishing, rock climbing, canoeing, dog sledding, snow rafting and kiting.

Festivals in Valdres

There are festivals in Valdres throughout the whole year. You can visit large sports events, food and farm festivals, but most of all music festivals that span from rock over folk music to classical.
